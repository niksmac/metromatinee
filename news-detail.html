Jomonte Suvisheshangal And Munthiri Vallikal Thalirkumbol To Release On January 19th!

Thu, 2017-01-12 18:42

Looks like confusions regarding the release dates of Malayalam movies are still continuing! As per the latest update, Dulquer Salmaan starrer Jomonte Suvisheshangal and Mohanlal starrer Munthiri Vallikal Thalirkumbol will be released on the same date. According to the latest report on Manorama news, Jomonte Suvisheshangal and Munthiri Vallikal Thalirkumbol will lock horns on January 19th. With the overwhelming response for Vijay's Bairavaa in Kerala theatres (that do not come under the control of Federation) the producers and distributors have now decided to release the movies that were initially slated for Christmas.

Inside sources say that during the release of Bairavaa, many members from the Federation expressed their willingness to screen the film and so, whichever theatre agrees to screen the films will be given the movie, provided the revenue share remains the same old proportion of 60:40. As the A-Class theatres in the state have been indefinitely shut from today, the movies will be released only in government owned/controlled theatres (over 200) in the state. The release dates of Jayasurya's Fukri and Prithviraj's Ezra will also be officially announced soon.
